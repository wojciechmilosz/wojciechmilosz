## 🚤 SeaSentinel (SimLE Science Club) - Autonomous Surface Vehicle
**Role:** Electrical Team Leader & Hardware Designer

As the Electrical Team Leader for the SeaSentinel project, I co-designed the complete electrical architecture for a small autonomous surface vehicle (ASV).  

A core component of this system is a custom-designed PCBs engineered to ensure the reliability and safety of the vehicle during international competitions.

**Key Hardware Features:**
* **Power Distribution & Handling:** Designed high-power routing to safely manage the current demands of the ASV's thrusters and core electronics.
* **Battery Management:** Implemented custom circuitry for safe power delivery and monitoring of the main battery packs.
* **System Monitoring:** Integrated diagnostic features to track vital electrical parameters in real-time.

**Technologies used:** KiCad, microROS, C++, Pi Pico, STM32, Autodesk Inventor.
